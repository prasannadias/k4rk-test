Refurbishing and Reusing

Your donations of computer equipment 4 R Kids is so important. It helps in the reduction of the "digital divide" by enabling equal access to technology to needy children throughout Southern California.

When a pick up needs to be scheduled please email, or fill out our Pick Up Request Form or email us at k4rk@k4rk.org

We accept old… Circuit Cards, Cabling, Computer Monitors, Computers, Printers, Computer Peripherals, Copiers, Fax Machines, DVD Players, Radios, Stereos …any electronic device … no TVs, broken glass, paint, toxic chemicals/materials or unsafe-to-handle items, please.

No household appliances please …Refrigerators, Stoves, Washers, Dryers, Vacuum Cleaners, Toasters, Blenders, etc. are not considered e-waste.

Komputers 4 R Kids will salvage what we can for children, and transfer what's left to a State Licensed Recycler. Help us help our children by passing on the information about our program-- this is a win-win situation for all involved! We offer a tax deductible receipt for donations, needy children are provided for, less e-waste accumulates in our land fills and it provides a safe and proper way to handle material at the end of its life cycle.
