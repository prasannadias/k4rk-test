Parts and Services

At Komputers 4 R Kids, we do so much more then provide computers to those who need them most, we also educate. We do so by teaching our volunteers how to refurbish, maintain, and repair computers. We are now offering our services once again to those that need it most, in a Student Repair Program. In this program, our supervised volunteers will learn to repair our customers computers, allowing them to gain real world experience.

For information on the services we offer, and pricing, please contact us at k4rk@k4rk.org
