Our Mission

Our mission is to help children of all ages acquire the technology and technology skills they need to succeed in the future by providing equitable access to technology resources.

The Komputers 4 R Kids Program has been designed to address four key issues:

  • E-waste
  • Computer to student ratio
  • Work experience for Information Technology students
  • PC’s for undeserved students due to economic, language and cultural barriers
