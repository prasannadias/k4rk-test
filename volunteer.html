Learn To Build A Computer

Komputers 4 R Kids has learning opporntunities for volunteers. Check out some of the pictures of the volunteers who are making a difference in our community. The reasons for participating vary from person to person. High school and vocational students may participate to get hands-on experience in repair, web site design or networking, whereas seniors may want to learn how to communicate over the Internet, keep up with the grandkids and stay current. Whatever your motivation may be, we invite you to participate and play an active role in the education of our children.
