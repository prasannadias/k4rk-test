Protection on the Internet

The Internet is a valuable tool for adults and children alike. It plays a major role in business, research and entertainment. Because of its increasing presence in the workplace, it is really beneficial for children, at any age, to become familiar with what the Internet is and how to use it.

So, we want our children to learn how to use the Internet and benefit from all the good it has to offer. Many parents, however, find themselves with the dilemma of trying to protect their children's safety while they are online. How do you do it? Well, first and foremost, you want to install a content filter on the computer that your child will be using.

Please use the links on the right to learn more about protecting your child and reduce the cost of protection by using the free resources available.
